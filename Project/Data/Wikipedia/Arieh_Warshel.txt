He is a pioneer in computational studies on functional properties of biological molecules, Distinguished Professor of Chemistry and Biochemistry, and holds the Dana and David Dornsife Chair in Chemistry at the [[University of Southern California]]. Warshel served in the Israeli Armored Corps. After serving the Israeli Army (final rank [[Captain (armed forces)|Captain]]), Warshel attended the [[Technion]], Haifa, where he received his BSc degree in chemistry, Summa Cum Laude, in 1966. Subsequently, he earned both MSc and PhD degrees in Chemical Physics (in 1967 and 1969,  respectively), with [[Shneior Lifson]] at [[Weizmann Institute of Science]], Israel.<ref>{{Cite journal |last=Levitt |first=Michael |date=2001-05-01 |title=The birth of computational structural biology |url=http://www.nature.com/doifinder/10.1038/87545 |journal=Nature Structural Biology |volume=8 |issue=5 |pages=392\u2013393 |doi=10.1038/87545|pmid=11323711 |s2cid=6519868 }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |date=2013-11-21 |title=A Nobel victory |url=https://news.usc.edu/57588/a-nobel-victory-for-arieh-warshel/ |access-date=2022-05-21 |website=USC News |language=en-US}}</ref> After his PhD, he did postdoctoral work at [[Harvard University]] until 1972, and from 1972 to 1976 he returned to the Weizmann Institute and worked for the [[Laboratory of Molecular Biology]], Cambridge, England. After being denied tenure by Weizmann Institute in 1976,<ref>[https://www.ynetnews.com/articles/0,7340,L-4438751,00.html Nobel laureate: We left because Warshel didn't get tenure in Israel] by Yitzhak Benhorin, October 9, 2013, [[YNET]]</ref> he joined the faculty of the Department of Chemistry at USC. He was awarded the 2013 [[Nobel Prize in Chemistry]].\n\nAs a soldier, he fought in both the 1967 [[Six-Day War]] and the [[1973 Yom Kippur War]], attaining the rank of captain in the [[Israel Defense Forces|IDF]].<ref>[http://www.timesofisrael.com/israeli-prof-arieh-warshel-shares-2013-nobel-prize-in-chemistry 3 Jewish professors \u2014 two of them Israeli \u2014 share 2013 Nobel Prize in
