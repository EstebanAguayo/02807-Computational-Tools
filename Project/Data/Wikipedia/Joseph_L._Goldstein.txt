Goldstein, who owned a clothing store. Goldstein received his BSci from [[Washington and Lee University]] in 1962, and his MD from the University of Texas [[University of Texas Southwestern Medical Center|Southwestern Medical School]] in 1966.<ref name=\"NobelFaces\" /> Upon completion of his [[Residency (medicine)|residency]], Goldstein moved to the [[National Institutes of Health]] (NIH) in [[Bethesda, Maryland]], where he worked in [[Molecular biology|biochemical genetics]].<ref name=\"Tonse\">{{Cite journal | last1 = Raju | first1 = T.
