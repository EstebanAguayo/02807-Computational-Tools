He served in the [[Israel Defense Forces]] for six weeks in 1985. In 1986, he began teaching at [[Stanford University]], and since then has split his time between Israel and California.<ref name=\"haaretz.com\"/> He went on to gain a research fellowship at [[Gonville and Caius College, Cambridge]].\n\nFrom 1980 to 1987, he was Professor of Chemical Physics at the Weizmann Institute of Science, Rehovot.
