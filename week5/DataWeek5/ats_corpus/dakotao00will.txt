This is the fifth revision and enlargement of our Dakota Hymn Book. 
Almost at the beginning of the mission some few hymns were prepared. 
Their number has increased with the larger needs of the churches. Many 
of these hymns are the composition of the Dakotas themselves. Some of 
our tunes are also Dakota native airs. Thus this book has grown with the 
growth of the Dakota Mission, and is a token of the development of our 
Indian churches. 

We would here acknowledge the help given us by. James K. Murray of 
Andover, Mass., who has harmonized a number of the native airs, and has 
otherwise assisted in the musical part of this work. 

We are also indebted to the following publishers and authors for the 
five use of their copyrights : Biglow & Main, J. Church & Co., Oliver Dit- 
son & Co., Philip Phillips, A. S. Barnes & Co., George E. Kingsley, Bev. D. 
E. Jones, Eev. E. P. Parker, W. G. Fischer, and A. J. Abbey. George E. 
Kingsley has also favored us with a tune (Dakota) composed specially for 
this work.
